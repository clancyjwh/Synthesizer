Signal Explanation ℹ This explains in plain English why the Buy or Sell signal was generated. A Buy signal is generated when the quoted market price is below the average synthetic price calculated across all routes — meaning the matrix thinks the pair is undervalued. A Sell signal is generated when the quoted price is above the average synthetic price — meaning the matrix thinks it is overvalued. The signal reflects what the cross-rate mathematics implies, not a recommendation to trade.

What is BPS?

Basis points (BPS) measure how far a quoted price has moved from its synthetic fair value. 1 BPS = 0.01%. The larger the number, the bigger the disagreement.

BPS Range What it means
0 – 5 Noise, not meaningful
5 – 20 Worth watching
20 – 50 Notable disagreement
50+ Strong signal
